What are Water-Borne Diseases?

Water-borne diseases are caused by drinking or coming into contact with contaminated water. These diseases are especially common in areas with poor sanitation.

Common Water-Borne Diseases

  • Cholera
  • Typhoid
  • Dysentery
  • Hepatitis A
  • Giardiasis

Symptoms

  • Diarrhea
  • Vomiting
  • Fever
  • Dehydration
  • Abdominal pain

Prevention Tips

  • Drink clean, filtered or boiled water
  • Wash hands regularly
  • Use proper sanitation
  • Avoid street food and untreated water
  • Get vaccinated when possible

Water-borne diseases affect millions worldwide. Simple hygiene practices and access to clean water can prevent most cases.
